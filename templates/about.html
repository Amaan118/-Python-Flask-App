Get started with Your SGPA Data

SGPA is a number that a student gets for his performance in the semester exams. It is a grading system used by many educational institutions worldwide. It has slight modifications in various countries, depending on the respective educational systems. SGPA is obtained in a single semester.CGPA is referred to as a cumulative grade point average. It was introduced in the CCE or Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation System for Class X. CGPA gives the average grade point of the student performance in all the subjects excluding the additional Subject. The subject wise performance of the students is recorded in forms of Grades.
